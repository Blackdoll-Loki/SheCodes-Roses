I love
David Austin roses

There are such varieties in my collection

Lady of Shalott

Rich orange-red buds open to chalice-shaped blooms, filled with loosely arranged, orange petals. The surrounding outer petals are salmon-pink with beautifully contrasting golden-yellow undersides

Wildeve

Distinctly quartered, rosette blooms are produced on long arching branches. They are light pink with a pretty apricot hue, the outer petals paling to almost white. It forms a bushy, mounding shrub.

Pilgrim

This variety has particularly beautiful blooms. The buds open to reveal many petalled cups, which gradually form large, flat rosettes. The color is a very attractive shade of soft yellow, the petals paling prettily towards the edges.

Charles Darwin

The flowers are very full and rounded at first, later opening up to shallow cups. They are yellow in color, tending almost towards mustard, and have a strong, delicious fragrance, varying between soft floral Tea and pure lemon.

Shropshire Lad

Large, cupped, light peach-pink rosettes, with a fruity Tea scent. Vigorous, with plentiful dark green foliage.
